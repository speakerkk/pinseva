Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

At Pin Seva, we believe that searching for postal information should be private and secure. This policy explains our commitment to protecting your data while you use our Indian PIN code information service.

Data Collection

No Personal Data: Pin Seva does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

Search History: We do not store your search queries. When you look up a PIN code or location, the request is processed in real-time and is not saved to a permanent database.

Third-Party Services

Our search functionality utilizes the "Postal PIN Code API" to provide accurate results. While we do not track you, third-party hosting platforms or API providers may use standard web logs to ensure service stability. Our advertising and analytics partners may use cookies to serve personalized ads.

Updates to This Policy

As Pin Seva evolves, we may update this policy to reflect changes in our service. We encourage users to check this page periodically for any updates. Your continued use of the site signifies your acceptance of these terms.
